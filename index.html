Chapter 1: Parts of Speech (शब्द भेद)

There are 8 parts of speech in English:

  1. Noun (संज्ञा): Name of person, place, thing or idea.
    Example: Ravi is a good boy.
  2. Pronoun (सर्वनाम): Used in place of noun.
    Example: He is playing.
  3. Verb (क्रिया): Shows action or state.
    Example: He runs fast.
  4. Adjective (विशेषण): Describes a noun.
    Example: He is a brilliant student.
  5. Adverb (क्रिया विशेषण): Describes a verb/adjective.
    Example: She sings beautifully.
  6. Preposition (संबंध सूचक): Shows relationship.
    Example: The book is on the table.
  7. Conjunction (संयोजक): Connects words/clauses.
    Example: I like tea and coffee.
  8. Interjection (विस्मयादिबोधक): Sudden expression.
    Example: Wow! What a car!

Chapter 2: Tenses (काल)

Tense का मतलब है क्रिया का समय — यानी काम कब हुआ।

English में 3 प्रकार के Tense होते हैं:

  • Present (वर्तमान काल)
  • Past (भूतकाल)
  • Future (भविष्य काल)

1. Present Tense (वर्तमान काल)

Type Structure Example Hindi
SimpleSubject + V1 + s/esHe plays cricket.वह क्रिकेट खेलता है।
ContinuousSubject + is/am/are + V1 + ingShe is reading.वह पढ़ रही है।
PerfectSubject + has/have + V3I have eaten.मैंने खा लिया है।
Perfect ContinuousSubject + has/have been + V1 + ingThey have been working.वे काम कर रहे हैं।

2. Past Tense (भूतकाल)

Type Structure Example Hindi
SimpleSubject + V2He played cricket.उसने क्रिकेट खेला।
ContinuousSubject + was/were + V1 + ingShe was reading.वह पढ़ रही थी।
PerfectSubject + had + V3I had eaten.मैंने खा लिया था।
Perfect ContinuousSubject + had been + V1 + ingThey had been working.वे काम कर रहे थे।

3. Future Tense (भविष्य काल)
